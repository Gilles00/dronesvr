Delivery by drone is
One can only wait so long for drone delivery to become a thing, so LunaDrop was born out of the necessity to pick up where Amazon left off.

What?

LunaDrop is a comprehensive drone delivery system. Users visit our web app to place an order and select one of our designated delivery locations. Back at the hub we pack up the order and clip the box to one of our drones. The drone then flies autonomously from the hub to the chosen landing site, descends to release the package, and returns to the hub for the next order.
